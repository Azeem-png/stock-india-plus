# Production Blockers

The app is not truly production-ready until these are handled:

1. Licensed Indian market data provider
2. Licensed or permitted financial news ingestion
3. Source-grounded AI with citations and timestamps
4. Legal disclaimer and compliance review
5. Privacy policy and terms
6. Android signing and release setup
7. Crash logging / analytics / monitoring
8. Auth, watchlist persistence, alerts if needed
9. Cache + refresh policy for market hours
10. Hallucination guardrails for stock recommendations
